Joseph Ngooma Delivers Powerful Performance as Fans Pack Lugogo Cricket Oval for Gospel Night

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 12 July 2026 · English
📷 A large crowd at an outdoor gospel concert with stage lights

The air at Lugogo Cricket Oval vibrated with worship and celebration as renowned gospel artist Joseph Ngooma staged a highly successful live concert already being described as one of the highlights of the year so far. Thousands of fans streamed into the venue, filling the stands and field area to capacity.

From the opening praise songs to the powerful closing moments, Ngooma held the audience in rapt attention with his anointed delivery and heartfelt ministration, with many in the crowd singing along, raising hands, and recording moments on their phones.

Speaking to our team the following morning, a visibly delighted Ngooma said the response left him inspired. "The love I felt yesterday was overwhelming. When people come together to lift God's name, it reminds you why music is a calling. I am heading back to create fresh songs that will bless this generation," he shared.

Industry stakeholders in attendance noted the professional organisation, quality sound, and spiritual atmosphere that set the event apart, seeing it as proof that faith-based music commands a loyal and expanding audience even in a competitive entertainment landscape. One close collaborator told King Musah Media: "Nights like this encourage many upcoming artists that you don't have to compromise to succeed. The market is there for authentic gospel."

Organisers hint that this could be the beginning of a series of larger gospel events aimed at uniting believers through music, as social media lit up overnight with videos, testimonies, and congratulatory messages.
