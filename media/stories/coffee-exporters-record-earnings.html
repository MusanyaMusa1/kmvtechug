Uganda's Coffee Exporters Report Record Quarterly Earnings

MK
Musah Kironde
Published 08 July 2026 · English
📷 Coffee beans drying in the sun at a farm in Uganda

Uganda's coffee sector posted its strongest quarterly earnings on record, driven by a combination of higher global prices and increased export volumes, according to figures released this week.

The Uganda Coffee Development Authority says exporters shipped significantly more bags compared to the same period last year, with Robusta varieties leading the growth.

Farmers in the central and western regions say the improved prices have started to translate into better incomes, though some warn that rising input costs are eating into the gains.

Analysts caution that global price swings mean the sector's fortunes could shift quickly, and are calling for continued investment in local value addition.
