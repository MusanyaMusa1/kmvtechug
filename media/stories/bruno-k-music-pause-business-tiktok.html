Bruno K Opens Up About Music Career Pause to Focus on Business and TikTok Ventures

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 15 June 2026 · English
📷 A smartphone recording content in a casual setting

Popular singer Bruno K sat down with the media to explain his decision to put his music career on hold, citing the need to build other income streams through business and TikTok influencing.

The artiste, known for his smooth vocals and romantic ballads, revealed he recently opened a bar in Kisasi and is dedicating more time to digital content creation, insisting the pause is strategic rather than permanent.

"Music will always be part of me, but I realised I needed to diversify. The bar business is growing, and TikTok gives me direct connection with fans while creating opportunities," Bruno K explained.

A music producer friend noted: "Bruno is being smart. The industry is tough, and building multiple streams is wise. We expect him to return stronger." Fans expressed support while eagerly awaiting his comeback, streaming his older hits in solidarity.
