Construction of New Karuma Bridge Set to Begin in October 2026

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 13 July 2026 · English
📷 Bridge construction site over a river

The government has announced that construction of the new Karuma Bridge will commence in October 2026, a major infrastructure boost expected to ease transport along a key route.

The project forms part of ongoing efforts to improve road networks and support economic activity under the new national budget. Officials say the bridge will enhance connectivity, reduce travel times, and stimulate trade between regions.

The development aligns with broader infrastructure priorities aimed at supporting Uganda's growth and monetisation agenda, with preparatory works reportedly advancing smoothly.
