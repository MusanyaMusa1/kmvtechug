Kizza Besigye Treason Trial Resumes Amid Lawyer Intimidation Claims

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 13 July 2026 · English
📷 Security personnel outside a courthouse entrance

The High Court treason trial of veteran opposition leader Dr. Kizza Besigye and his co-accused has resumed under tight security, with fresh reports of challenges facing the defence team.

Justice Emmanuel Baguma has ordered state-funded lawyers after several defence counsel faced reported intimidation, abduction, or denial of entry into the country. Besigye has told the court he appeared under duress, while prosecutors continue presenting evidence in a case that has drawn significant public attention.

Court sessions have been marked by procedural disputes, including demands for full evidence disclosure. The trial is seen as a major test for Uganda's justice system and political landscape.
