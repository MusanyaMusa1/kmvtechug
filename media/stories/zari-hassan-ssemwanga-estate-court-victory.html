Landmark Victory for Zari Hassan and Sons as Court Resolves Ivan Ssemwanga Estate Battle

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 08 July 2026 · English
📷 A courtroom gavel resting on a wooden desk

Socialite and businesswoman Zari Hassan, together with her sons, celebrated a significant court victory as the long-running dispute over the estate of the late Ivan Ssemwanga reached a resolution in their favour.

The Family Court delivered a ruling that brings closure to years of legal wrangling, finally determining the distribution and management of the businessman's estate, with Zari and her children emerging as key beneficiaries in an emotional end to a battle that has played out publicly and affected family dynamics.

Representatives following the judgment expressed relief and gratitude that the matter can now move forward constructively, with the resolution expected to provide stability for the family and allow focus on legacy projects and business interests Ivan left behind.

An associate close to the family told King Musah Media: "This has been a difficult journey. Zari has shown strength throughout. Today's ruling allows healing and proper planning for the future." Legal experts note such resolutions often set precedents in estate matters involving blended families and complex inheritances in Uganda.
