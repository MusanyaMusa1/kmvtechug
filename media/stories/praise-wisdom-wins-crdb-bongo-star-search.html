Ugandan Vocalist Praise Wisdom Crowned Winner of CRDB Bongo Star Search in Regional Triumph

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 13 July 2026 · English
📷 A singer holding a trophy on stage with confetti

Huge pride swept across Uganda after talented vocalist Praise Wisdom won the prestigious CRDB Bongo Star Search competition, walking away with the top prize of TZS 50 million.

The victory marks a significant regional success for Ugandan talent, with Praise Wisdom beating strong competition from across East Africa. Judges and audiences praised her powerful voice, stage presence, and emotional delivery throughout the competition.

"This is for Uganda. I am grateful to everyone who voted and believed in me. This win opens new doors, and I am ready to work harder," she said with visible emotion, dedicating the achievement to her supporters and family.

One industry leader commented: "Praise Wisdom's victory boosts confidence for many upcoming artistes. It shows that with talent and preparation, Ugandans can compete and win at the highest level." The cash prize will support her music projects, with exposure expected to attract collaborations across East Africa.
