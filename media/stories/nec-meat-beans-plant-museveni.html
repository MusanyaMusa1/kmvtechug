NEC Meat and Beans Processing Plant to Be Commissioned by President Museveni

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 15 July 2026 · English
📷 Agro-processing factory equipment

President Yoweri Museveni is set to commission the National Enterprise Corporation meat and beans processing facility, a key agro-processing project aimed at adding value to local produce.

The plant is expected to boost farmers' incomes, create jobs, and support Uganda's push towards commercial agriculture under the current budget's economic monetisation theme, reflecting broader government efforts to strengthen the agro-industrial sector and reduce reliance on raw exports.

Commissioning is anticipated to take place soon, with local communities hopeful for positive economic impact.
