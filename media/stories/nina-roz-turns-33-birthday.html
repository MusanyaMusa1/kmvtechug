Birthday Cheers for Nina Roz as She Turns 33 Amid Growing Musical Influence

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 17 July 2026 · English
📷 A microphone on a stage with warm celebratory lighting

The Ugandan entertainment scene lit up with warm tributes and messages as talented singer and songwriter Nina Roz celebrated her 33rd birthday. Known for her distinctive voice, emotional depth in lyrics, and steady rise in the industry, she received an outpouring of love from fans, fellow artistes, family, and admirers across social media.

While the artist opted for a relatively private celebration with close family and friends, public messages flooded in praising her growth. One prominent artiste posted: "Happy 33rd, Nina! Your voice carries stories that many relate to. Keep blessing us with your gift."

Industry watchers note that Nina Roz enters this new year of life at an exciting phase, with her recent works still receiving airplay and her fan base expanding regionally. Her ability to blend genres while staying authentic has earned her respect among both peers and older listeners who appreciate thoughtful music.

Many in the entertainment community used the occasion to reflect on the role of women artistes in shaping Uganda's music narrative, with Nina Roz standing out as one of the consistent voices proving that talent, hard work, and authenticity yield lasting impact.
