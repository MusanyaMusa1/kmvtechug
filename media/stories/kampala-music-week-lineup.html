Kampala Music Week Announces Star-Studded Lineup

DA
Diana Achieng
Published 10 July 2026 · English
📷 A concert crowd with stage lights during a past Kampala Music Week event

Kampala Music Week returns next month with its most ambitious lineup to date, organisers announced on Thursday, featuring a mix of established Ugandan artists and emerging talent from across East Africa.

The three-day festival will take place at Lugogo, with organisers promising expanded food and craft vendor areas alongside the main stage.

"We wanted this edition to feel like a true celebration of homegrown talent," said the festival's creative director during the lineup announcement.

Early bird tickets go on sale this weekend, with organisers expecting them to sell out quickly given the demand from previous years.
