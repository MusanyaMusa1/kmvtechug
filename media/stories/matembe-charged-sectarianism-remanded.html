Former Minister Matembe Charged with Sectarianism and Remanded

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 01 July 2026 · English
📷 Luzira Prison gate in Kampala

Former minister Miria Matembe has been charged with sectarianism and remanded to Luzira Prison, in a case that has sparked public debate on political expression and unity.

Court proceedings are ongoing following her arrest, amid heightened political sensitivities in the country. Supporters and critics alike are closely watching how the justice system handles the matter.

Legal experts note that such cases often test the balance between maintaining national cohesion and protecting individual rights.
