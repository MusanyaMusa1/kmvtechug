Massive National Pride as Uganda's Ghetto Kids Set to Perform with Shakira at 2026 FIFA World Cup Final

KM
King Musah Media News Desk
Published 05 July 2026 · English
📷 A group of young dancers performing energetically

Uganda erupted in celebration after news broke that the talented dance group Ghetto Kids will share the stage with global superstar Shakira during the 2026 FIFA World Cup final in New Jersey.

The announcement triggered an outpouring of pride across the country, with many viewing it as a historic moment that puts Ugandan talent on the biggest sporting stage in the world. The Ghetto Kids, already known for their energetic performances and international collaborations, are set to dazzle a global audience of millions alongside the Colombian icon.

A representative for the Ghetto Kids expressed gratitude and excitement: "This is a dream come true. We thank God, our supporters, and everyone who believed in us. We will represent Uganda with full energy and make the country proud."

The performance comes as Uganda also prepares to co-host AFCON 2027, further boosting the nation's profile in sports and entertainment. Industry observers note that such high-profile international appearances help elevate Ugandan creative exports and inspire the next generation.
