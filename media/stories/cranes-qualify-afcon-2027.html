Uganda Cranes Qualify for AFCON 2027 After Dramatic Win

IS
Ivan Ssebunya
Published 13 July 2026 · English
📷 Uganda Cranes players celebrating on the pitch after scoring

The Uganda Cranes booked their ticket to AFCON 2027 on Sunday night with a 2-1 win in a qualifier that went down to the final minutes.

Substitute striker Joseph Mutumba scored the winning goal in the 89th minute, sparking wild celebrations among fans who had packed the stadium despite a nervy first half.

Head coach praised the squad's resilience, saying the team "never stopped believing" even after conceding an early equaliser.

This will be Uganda's second consecutive AFCON appearance, and fans are already looking ahead to the group stage draw expected later this year.

Local sports bars across Kampala reported a surge in customers watching the match, with celebrations continuing well into the night.
